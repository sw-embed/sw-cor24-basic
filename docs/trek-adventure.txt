



There was a game, in BASIC, at the back of a magazine, sometime around early 1980s, that was a Star Trek text adventure.  You awake on the bridge of the enterprise alone.  The computer says something about a decaying orbit, engines are down.  You have access to the turbolift to get to some parts of the ship.  You need to find an ID badge/card-key to access locked room(s), you need to find tools, and eventually fix the engine to stop the decaying orbit.  There are also Klingons taking over at some point.  You can thwart the Klingons by throwing a trible at them.  Can you produce a BASIC program to do something like this?

How it plays
A successful path is roughly:

TAKE PHASER on the bridge

GO TURBOLIFT

GO CORRIDOR

GO QUARTERS

TAKE CARD

back to corridor

GO STORAGE

TAKE TOOLKIT

TAKE COUPLER

TAKE TRIBBLE

eventually Klingons appear

if cornered in the shuttle bay area, THROW TRIBBLE

go to engineering

USE TOOLKIT

USE COUPLER

Risks / compatibility notes
UCASE$ exists in QBasic and many later BASICs, but some very old BASICs do not have it.

If your BASIC lacks UCASE$, I can rewrite the parser to avoid it.

Some dialects dislike long variable names like TK or EC; most later Microsoft BASICs are fine, but I can also convert this to stricter TRS-80 / Applesoft / vintage magazine style.
